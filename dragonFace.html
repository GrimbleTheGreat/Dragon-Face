How to Play Dragon Face

Objective

Capture the opponent's Emperor to win the game.

The Pieces

  • (E) Emperor: Moves one space in any direction.
  • (A) Ambassador: Moves any number of spaces in any direction (like a queen).
  • (G) Governor: Moves one space forward (straight or diagonally) and the first move it makes is a double!

Key Rules

  • Capture: Jump over an opponent's piece to capture it. The captured piece is not removed; it flips to your color and becomes yours!
  • Immunity: A newly flipped piece cannot be re-captured on the opponent's very next turn.
  • Sacrifice Zone: The outer border of the board. Be careful when moving pieces here! Your pieces can fall into the Sacrifice Zone when capturing an enemy piece. Governors that land in the sacrifice zone will remain for the rest of the game! Ambassadors remain in the sacrifice zone until a governor reaches the backline (NOT CROSSES).
